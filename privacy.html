Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 13, 2025

Introduction

Emulation Helper Bot ("we", "us", or "our") is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our Discord bot ("the Bot") or visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Discord User Data: User IDs, usernames, and server IDs to facilitate command execution and server interactions.
  • Command Usage: Information about commands used (e.g., /ban, /poll) to ensure functionality and monitor usage patterns.
  • Messages: Temporary processing of message content for specific commands (e.g., /say), not stored persistently.
  • Contact Form Data: Name, email, and message content submitted via our website’s contact form.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Enable and enhance the Bot’s functionality (e.g., executing commands like /mute or /eventcreate).
  • Support server moderation, event management, and community engagement features.
  • Respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • Analyze usage to improve performance and add features.

Data Storage and Security

Data is processed in-memory and not stored unless necessary (e.g., for an economy system with a database, if implemented). We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, though no system is entirely secure.

Third-Party Services

The Bot operates using Discord’s API, subject to their Privacy Policy. Contact form data may be processed by Formspree (see their Privacy Policy).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we process.
  • Opt out by removing the Bot from your server or not using it.
  • Contact us with privacy concerns via the website’s contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy, with changes posted here and the date updated.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns, use the contact form on our website or join our Discord support server.
